What's Included

Every Component,
Professionally Cleaned

We disassemble, degrease and reassemble every part of your oven using professional-grade, bio-degradable products — safe for use immediately after cleaning, no harsh fumes.

Oven Interior

Full interior degreasing including the oven walls, ceiling, floor and element area. Carbonised grease and baked-on residue completely removed.

Oven Door & Glass

The door is fully disassembled and each glass panel is separately cleaned to a crystal-clear finish — removing grease that's impossible to reach from the outside.

Shelves & Racks

All oven shelves and wire racks are removed and deep-cleaned using a dip-tank method, removing all burnt-on grease and restoring a like-new finish.

Hob & Burners

Hob surface, burner caps, pan supports and control panel all cleaned and degreased. Ceramic, induction and gas hobs all catered for.

BBQ Cleaning

Full BBQ deep clean including grill grates, burners, grease trays and exterior. Leave it ready for the next summer get-together.

Safe Products

All products used are bio-degradable, non-caustic and food-safe. No harsh chemical smells — your oven is ready to use the same day.

FAQs

Common Questions About
Oven & BBQ Cleaning

How long does an oven clean take?
A single oven typically takes 1.5–2.5 hours depending on the level of soiling. A double oven or range cooker may take 3–4 hours. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when you book.
Can I use my oven straight after cleaning?
Yes — all products we use are bio-degradable and food-safe. Once we've finished and your oven is reassembled, it's ready to use immediately. We recommend a short 10-minute warm-up on first use to fully cure.
Do you clean all oven types?
Yes — we clean all types including single electric ovens, double ovens, gas ovens, range cookers (AGA, Rangemaster, Smeg etc.), fan-assisted ovens and combination microwave ovens.
Can you clean the extractor fan too?
Yes — extractor fan and filter cleaning is available as an add-on to any oven clean from just £25. This includes cleaning the mesh filter, fan casing and any accessible duct work.
Do you cover Epsom, Sutton and surrounding areas?
Yes — we provide oven and BBQ cleaning throughout Epsom, Sutton, Wimbledon, Kingston, Cheam, Banstead, Ewell and all surrounding areas within 15 miles. Get in touch to confirm your postcode.
