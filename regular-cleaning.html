Dusting surfaces, vacuuming, mopping hard floors, cleaning mirrors and tidying throughout all living areas and hallways.

FAQs

Common Questions About
Regular Cleaning

How often should I book a regular clean?
Most customers choose weekly or fortnightly visits. We recommend weekly if you have children or pets, and fortnightly for smaller households or those who work from home less frequently. We'll recommend the best frequency based on your home size and lifestyle.
Will I always get the same cleaner?
Yes. We believe consistency builds trust. You'll be assigned a dedicated cleaner who will get to know your home, your preferences, and your standards — so every visit feels personal and thorough.
Do I need to provide cleaning products?
We can bring all professional-grade products and equipment, or use your own preferred products — whichever you prefer. Just let us know when booking and we'll sort the rest.
Is there a minimum booking length?
We typically recommend a minimum of 2 hours per visit to do a thorough job. For larger homes, we'll advise on the appropriate time needed during your free quote.
Do you cover Epsom, Sutton and Wimbledon?
Yes — we provide regular house cleaning throughout Epsom, Sutton, Wimbledon, Kingston, Cheam, Banstead, Ewell and all surrounding areas within a 15-mile radius. Get in touch to confirm your postcode.
