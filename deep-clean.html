FAQs

Common Questions About
Deep & End of Tenancy Cleans

What's the difference between a deep clean and an end of tenancy clean?
An end of tenancy clean is specifically structured to meet letting agency and landlord check-out standards, including inventories. A deep clean is a thorough top-to-bottom clean for any property — ideal for a seasonal reset or moving in. Both follow the same comprehensive checklist.
Is the deposit-back guarantee real?
Absolutely. If your landlord or letting agent raises a cleaning issue from our visit, contact us within 72 hours and we will return to re-clean the flagged areas free of charge. We're confident in our standards — this rarely happens.
Does the deep clean include oven cleaning?
The deep clean includes the oven exterior and hob. For a full professional oven degreasing (inside the oven, racks, door glass, and oven trays), we recommend adding our dedicated Oven Cleaning service. This can be booked at the same time for a combined visit.
How long does a deep clean take?
A typical 2-bedroom property takes around 4–6 hours. Larger properties may require a full day or a two-person team. We'll give you an accurate estimate when you book your free quote.
Do you serve Epsom, Sutton and Wimbledon for end of tenancy cleans?
Yes — we provide end of tenancy and deep cleaning throughout Epsom, Sutton, Wimbledon, Kingston, Cheam, Banstead, Ewell and all surrounding areas within 15 miles. Contact us to confirm your postcode.
