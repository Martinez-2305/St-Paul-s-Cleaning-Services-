FAQs

Common Questions About
Carpet Cleaning

How long does carpet cleaning take?
Typically 45–60 minutes per room, depending on size and soiling. A 3-bedroom house (carpeted throughout) usually takes around 2.5–3.5 hours including drying time.
How long until the carpets are dry?
Most carpets dry within 2–4 hours. Thicker or denser piles may take up to 6 hours. Good ventilation speeds the process significantly — we'll advise you on opening windows after the clean.
Can you remove pet stains and odours?
Yes — we use specialist enzyme-based treatments for pet urine and odours that neutralise at source rather than just masking the smell. Most pet stains are significantly improved or fully removed.
Is hot water extraction safe for all carpets?
Hot water extraction is safe for the vast majority of carpet types including wool, synthetic, nylon and polypropylene. We assess your carpet before starting and will advise if a different method is better suited.
Do you offer upholstery cleaning too?
Yes — upholstery cleaning for sofas, armchairs and fabric headboards is available as an add-on. Ask about pricing when you request your free quote.
Do you cover Epsom, Sutton and Wimbledon for carpet cleaning?
Yes — we provide professional carpet cleaning throughout Epsom, Sutton, Wimbledon, Kingston, Cheam, Banstead, Ewell and all surrounding areas within 15 miles of Epsom, Surrey.
